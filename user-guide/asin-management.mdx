# ASIN Management

The ASIN Management section allows you to add, monitor, and manage all your Amazon product listings in one centralized location.

## Monitored ASINs Dashboard

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/monitored-asins.png" alt="Monitored ASINs Dashboard" />
</Frame>

The Monitored ASINs dashboard serves as your central hub for managing all products being tracked by Alertix.

### Dashboard Features

- **Section Header**: "Monitored ASINs" with count indicator (e.g., "5 ASINs monitored")
- **Upload ASINs Button**: For bulk addition of products
- **Search Field**: "Search ASINs..." for finding specific products
- **Filter Tabs**: Sort by All, Active, or Inactive status
- **Sort Dropdown**: Arrange ASINs by date created, alphabetically, or by alert count

### Product Cards

Each monitored ASIN is displayed as a card containing:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Visual Elements">
    - Product image
    - Product title (truncated if too long)
    - ASIN code (e.g., B0D94YLWJ6)
    - Status indicator ("Active" or "Inactive")
  </Card>
  <Card title="Alert Information">
    - New alert count
    - In-progress alert count
    - Historical alert data
    - "View Details" button for accessing ASIN-specific information
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Hover over a truncated product title to see the full name in a tooltip.
</Tip>

## Adding ASINs

Alertix provides multiple ways to add products for monitoring:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Individual ASIN">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click Add ASIN">
        Select the "Add ASIN" button in the top-right corner of the Monitored ASINs dashboard.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter ASIN">
        Type or paste the Amazon ASIN code (10-character alphanumeric identifier).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Product Details (Optional)">
        You can add notes or tags to help organize your products.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save">
        Click "Save" to begin monitoring the ASIN.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Bulk Upload">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click Upload ASINs">
        Select the "Upload ASINs" button in the Monitored ASINs dashboard.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Prepare CSV File">
        Create a CSV file with your ASINs (template available for download).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Upload File">
        Drag and drop your CSV file or click to browse and select it.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review and Confirm">
        Verify the detected ASINs and click "Confirm" to add them all.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bulk-upload.png" alt="Bulk ASIN Upload" />
    </Frame>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  Alertix has a maximum limit of ASINs you can monitor based on your subscription plan. Check your plan details before adding new ASINs.
</Warning>

## ASIN Details View

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/asin-details-view.png" alt="ASIN Details View" />
</Frame>

Clicking "View Details" on any ASIN card opens the detailed view for that specific product.

### Available Information

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Product Information">
    - Full product title
    - ASIN code
    - Product description
    - Current Amazon listing URL
    - Product image
    - Category information
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Monitoring Status">
    - Active/Inactive status
    - Monitoring start date
    - Last checked timestamp
    - Alert history timeline
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Alert Preferences">
    - ASIN-specific alert settings
    - Override toggles for global preferences
    - Custom notification settings
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Performance Metrics">
    - Alert frequency over time
    - Common alert types for this product
    - Comparative data with similar ASINs
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Actions Available

From the ASIN details view, you can:

- **Edit Product Details**: Update notes, tags, or other custom information
- **Configure Alerts**: Set ASIN-specific alert preferences
- **View Alert History**: See a timeline of all past alerts for this product
- **Pause Monitoring**: Temporarily stop monitoring this ASIN
- **Remove ASIN**: Permanently remove this product from monitoring
- **View on Amazon**: Open the product listing directly on Amazon

<Info>
  Changes made to ASIN-specific alert preferences override your global alert settings only for that particular product.
</Info>

## ASIN Limits and Recommendations

Your subscription plan determines how many ASINs you can monitor:

| Plan | ASIN Limit | Recommended For |
|------|------------|-----------------|
| Basic | 25 ASINs | Small sellers with limited catalog |
| Professional | 100 ASINs | Mid-sized sellers with moderate catalog |
| Business | 500 ASINs | Large sellers with extensive catalog |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | High-volume sellers and agencies |
