## Amazon Seller Account Connection

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/amazon-connection-settings.png" alt="Amazon Connection Settings" />
</Frame>

Manage your connection to Amazon Seller Central to ensure Alertix can properly monitor your listings.

### Connection Management

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Current Connection">
    See your currently connected Amazon Seller ID and associated brand name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Connection">
    Update your Amazon Seller ID if it has changed or if you need to monitor a different account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Additional Accounts">
    Connect multiple Amazon Seller accounts if you operate across different regions or brands (available on Business and Enterprise plans).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Connection">
    Test your connection to ensure Alertix can properly access your listing data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Amazon Seller ID Guidance

If you need help finding your Amazon Seller ID:

1. Log in to Amazon Seller Central
2. Navigate to Settings â†’ Account Info
3. Your Seller ID is listed under "Business Information"

<Info>
  For optimal monitoring, ensure your Amazon Seller ID is correctly entered and that you've provided the primary brand name associated with your account.
</Info>

## Subscription Management

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/subscription-management.png" alt="Subscription Management" />
</Frame>

Manage your Alertix subscription plan, billing information, and payment history.

### Plan Information

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Current Plan" icon="crown">
    - Plan name and tier
    - Monthly/annual billing cycle
    - ASIN monitoring limit
    - Feature access
    - Renewal date
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Billing Details" icon="credit-card">
    - Payment method
    - Billing address
    - Invoice history
    - Receipt downloads
    - Tax information
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Plan Management

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Upgrade Plan">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Compare Plans">
        Review available plans and their features to find the best fit for your business.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select New Plan">
        Choose the plan that meets your needs based on ASIN count and feature requirements.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm Billing Changes">
        Review the prorated charges or credits for your billing cycle.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete Upgrade">
        Confirm your selection to immediately upgrade your account.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Downgrade Plan">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Review Limitations">
        Understand what features or ASIN capacity you'll lose by downgrading.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select New Plan">
        Choose which lower-tier plan you want to switch to.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm Changes">
        Acknowledge any potential data loss or feature restrictions.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Schedule Downgrade">
        Your plan will change at the end of your current billing cycle.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  Downgrading your plan may result in the loss of monitoring capacity if you exceed the ASIN limit of your new plan. You'll need to remove excess ASINs before the downgrade takes effect.
</Warning>

## Account Deletion

If you need to delete your Alertix account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Export Your Data">
    Before deletion, download any important data you want to keep, including alert history and ASIN information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel Subscription">
    Ensure your subscription is canceled to prevent future billing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request Deletion">
    Contact support or use the "Delete Account" option in Account Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Verify your identity and confirm that you understand all data will be permanently removed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including alert history and ASIN configurations, will be permanently removed from our systems.
</Warning>
