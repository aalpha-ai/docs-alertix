## Account Settings

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/settings.png" alt="Account Settings" />
</Frame>

The Account Settings page provides access to all your personal account configuration options.

### Personal Information

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Profile Settings">
    - Update your name - Change your email address - Modify your password - Set
    your time zone - Configure language preferences
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Security Settings">
    - Enable/disable two-factor authentication - View active sessions - Manage
    connected devices - Set up security questions - Review account activity logs
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Notification Preferences">
    - Configure email notification frequency - Enable/disable mobile push
    notifications - Set up SMS alerts for critical changes - Customize
    notification digest settings - Configure quiet hours
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  Changing your email address will require verification of the new address
  before the change takes effect.
</Warning>

## Amazon Seller Account Connection

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/amazon-connect.png" alt="Amazon Connection Settings" />
</Frame>

Manage your connection to Amazon Seller Central to ensure Alertix can properly monitor your listings.

### Connection Management

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Current Connection">
    See your currently connected Amazon Seller ID and associated brand name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Connection">
    Update your Amazon Seller ID if it has changed or if you need to monitor a
    different account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Additional Accounts">
    Connect multiple Amazon Seller accounts if you operate across different
    regions or brands (available on Business and Enterprise plans).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Connection">
    Test your connection to ensure Alertix can properly access your listing
    data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account Deletion

If you need to delete your Alertix account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Export Your Data">
    Before deletion, download any important data you want to keep, including
    alert history and ASIN information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel Subscription">
    Ensure your subscription is canceled to prevent future billing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request Deletion">
    Contact support or use the "Delete Account" option in Account Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Verify your identity and confirm that you understand all data will be
    permanently removed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including
  alert history and ASIN configurations, will be permanently removed from our
  systems.
</Warning>
