# Marketing Preferences

Alertix provides valuable marketing communications to help you get the most out of the platform. This section allows you to manage your marketing preferences and subscription settings.

## Marketing Alerts Card

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/marketing-alerts.png" alt="Marketing Alerts Card" />
</Frame>

The Marketing Alerts section allows you to control what types of promotional and educational content you receive from Alertix.

### Available Options

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Marketing Materials">
    Toggle to receive promotional content about Alertix features, updates, and special offers. This includes:
    - New feature announcements
    - Special promotions and discounts
    - Platform updates and improvements
    - Seasonal offers and campaigns
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Educational Content">
    Toggle to receive educational resources to help you maximize your success on Amazon:
    - Best practices for Amazon sellers
    - Industry trends and insights
    - Case studies and success stories
    - Webinar invitations and training opportunities
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Product Updates">
    Toggle to receive notifications about Alertix product updates:
    - New feature releases
    - Platform improvements
    - Bug fixes and performance enhancements
    - Roadmap previews
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
  You can update your marketing preferences at any time without affecting your critical alert notifications for your monitored ASINs.
</Info>

## Subscription Management

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/subscription-preferences.png" alt="Subscription Preferences" />
</Frame>

Manage your email subscription preferences to control the frequency and types of communications you receive.

### Email Preferences

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Frequency">
    Choose how often you receive marketing communications:
    - Daily digest
    - Weekly newsletter
    - Monthly updates
    - Special announcements only
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Content Types">
    Select the specific types of content you're interested in:
    - Amazon selling tips
    - Alertix feature highlights
    - Industry news and trends
    - Promotional offers
    - Webinars and events
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Format">
    Choose your preferred email format:
    - HTML (rich media)
    - Plain text
    - Mobile-optimized
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
