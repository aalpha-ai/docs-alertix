## Troubleshooting Account Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Login Problems">
    If you can't log in: - Use the "Forgot password" option - Check for correct
    email address - Verify 2FA setup if enabled - Clear browser cache and
    cookies
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Email Verification">
    If you're not receiving verification emails: - Check your spam/junk folder -
    Verify the email address is correct - Add clerk.com to your safe senders
    list - Try an alternate email address
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="2FA Issues">
    If you're having trouble with two-factor authentication: - Ensure your
    device time is synced correctly - Use backup codes if available - Contact
    support if you're locked out
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Billing Access">
    If you can't access the billing portal: - Ensure you're signed in with the
    correct account - Check if your subscription is active - Try clearing
    cookies and cache - Contact support for direct assistance
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Most account issues can be resolved through the self-service options in your
  profile. If you need additional help, our support team is available through
  the Help Center.
</Tip>
