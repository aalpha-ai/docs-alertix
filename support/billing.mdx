## Managing Your Subscription

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/subscription-management.png" alt="Subscription Management" />
</Frame>

### Viewing Your Current Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Account Settings">
    Navigate to Account Settings in the main navigation menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Subscription Tab">
    Click on the "Subscription" tab to view your current plan details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Plan Information">
    Here you can see your current plan, billing cycle, next payment date, and usage statistics.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Upgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Subscription Settings">
    Navigate to Account Settings → Subscription.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Upgrade">
    Click the "Upgrade Plan" button to view available upgrade options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose New Plan">
    Select your desired plan from the available options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Upgrade">
    Review the prorated cost information and confirm your upgrade.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Payment Information">
    If needed, update your payment information before finalizing the upgrade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  When upgrading, you'll only be charged the prorated difference between your current plan and the new plan for the remainder of your billing cycle.
</Info>

### Downgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Subscription Settings">
    Navigate to Account Settings → Subscription.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Downgrade">
    Click the "Downgrade Plan" button to view available options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose New Plan">
    Select your desired plan from the available options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Limitations">
    Review any feature or capacity limitations that will apply after downgrading.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Downgrade">
    Confirm your downgrade selection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Plan downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. If you exceed the limits of your new plan (e.g., monitored ASINs), you'll need to reduce usage before the downgrade takes effect.
</Warning>

## Billing Policies

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Billing Cycles">
    Alertix subscriptions are billed on a monthly or annual basis:
    
    - **Monthly subscriptions** are charged on the same day each month
    - **Annual subscriptions** are charged once per year and include a 20% discount compared to monthly billing
    
    Your billing date is determined by the date you initially subscribed.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Proration">
    When upgrading your plan mid-cycle, we calculate a prorated charge based on:
    
    1. The remaining days in your current billing cycle
    2. The price difference between your current and new plan
    
    This prorated amount is charged immediately, and your regular billing cycle continues with the new rate.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Refund Policy">
    - **Monthly plans**: We do not offer refunds for partial months
    - **Annual plans**: Refunds may be available on a prorated basis within 30 days of purchase
    - **Enterprise plans**: Refund policies are defined in your service agreement
    
    For special circumstances, please contact our billing support team.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  Cancellation of your subscription will result in immediate loss of access to premium features. Any data associated with your account will be retained for 30 days after cancellation, after which it may be permanently deleted.
</Warning>

## Usage Limits & Overage

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/usage-limits.png" alt="Usage Limits" />
</Frame>

Each subscription plan includes specific usage limits:

### ASIN Monitoring Limits

| Plan | ASIN Limit | Monitoring Frequency |
|------|------------|----------------------|
| Basic | 25 ASINs | Every 12 hours |
| Professional | 100 ASINs | Every 6 hours |
| Business | 500 ASINs | Every 3 hours |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | Customizable (as frequent as hourly) |

### Team Member Limits

| Plan | Team Members |
|------|--------------|
| Basic | 1 user |
| Professional | 2 users |
| Business | 5 users |
| Enterprise | Unlimited |

<Info>
  You can view your current usage statistics in the Account Settings → Subscription dashboard.
</Info>

### Handling Overage

If you approach your plan's limits, you'll receive notifications prompting you to:

1. Upgrade to a higher tier plan
2. Reduce usage to stay within your current limits
3. Contact sales for a custom plan (Business and Enterprise only)

<Warning>
  If you exceed your ASIN limit, new ASINs cannot be added until you either upgrade your plan or remove existing ASINs from monitoring.
</Warning>

## Cancellation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Subscription Settings">
    Navigate to Account Settings → Subscription.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Cancel Subscription">
    Click the "Cancel Subscription" button at the bottom of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Feedback">
    Optionally share your reason for cancellation to help us improve our service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Cancellation">
    Confirm that you want to cancel your subscription.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Confirmation">
    You'll receive an email confirming your cancellation and the date your access will end.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  After cancellation:
  - Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period
  - Your data will be retained for 30 days after your access ends
  - You can reactivate your account within this 30-day window without data loss
</Warning>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I change my billing cycle from monthly to annual?">
    To switch from monthly to annual billing:
    
    1. Navigate to Account Settings → Subscription
    2. Click "Switch to Annual Billing"
    3. Review the new pricing (including the 20% discount)
    4. Confirm the change
    
    You'll be charged for a full year immediately, and your next billing date will be set to one year from today.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I need to add more ASINs than my plan allows?">
    If you need to monitor more ASINs than your current plan allows, you have two options:
    
    1. Upgrade to the next plan tier that accommodates your needs
    2. Contact sales for a custom plan if you're already on a Business plan or need a solution between standard tiers
    
    We recommend regularly reviewing your monitored ASINs and removing any that are no longer relevant to stay within your plan limits.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I switch between plans at any time?">
    Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time, and the change takes effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer discounts for agencies or sellers with multiple accounts?">
    Yes, we offer agency pricing for partners managing multiple seller accounts. Contact our sales team at agencies@alertix.com to discuss agency pricing options.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
