# Security & Data Protection

At Alertix, we prioritize the security of your account and the protection of your data. This page outlines our security measures and best practices to help you maintain a secure experience.

## Authentication Security

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/authentication-security.png" alt="Authentication Security" />
</Frame>

Alertix implements robust authentication mechanisms to protect your account:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Secure Authentication" icon="lock">
    We use Clerk, a trusted authentication provider, to handle all login processes securely with industry-standard encryption.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Two-Factor Authentication" icon="shield-check">
    Enable 2FA for an additional layer of security, requiring both your password and a verification code to access your account.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Session Management" icon="user-clock">
    Monitor and manage all active sessions, with the ability to terminate any session from unknown devices.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Password Policies" icon="key">
    Strong password requirements and secure password hashing ensure your credentials remain protected.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Enabling Two-Factor Authentication

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Security Settings">
    Navigate to Account Settings â†’ Security to find the two-factor authentication options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable 2FA">
    Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication" and choose your preferred method (authenticator app or SMS).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Verification">
    Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your chosen verification method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Backup Codes">
    Store your backup codes in a secure location to ensure you can access your account if you lose your primary 2FA device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If you lose access to your two-factor authentication device and don't have backup codes, account recovery may require additional verification steps and support assistance.
</Warning>

## Data Protection

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/data-protection.png" alt="Data Protection" />
</Frame>

We implement comprehensive measures to protect your sensitive information:

### Data Security Measures

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Encryption">
    - All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2+
    - Sensitive data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
    - Database encryption for all personally identifiable information
    - Secure key management practices
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Infrastructure Security">
    - Cloud infrastructure hosted on AWS with multiple security layers
    - Regular security audits and penetration testing
    - Network segmentation and firewall protection
    - DDoS protection and mitigation systems
    - 24/7 infrastructure monitoring
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Access Controls">
    - Role-based access control for all system components
    - Principle of least privilege for all staff access
    - Multi-factor authentication for administrative access
    - Comprehensive audit logging of all system access
    - Regular access review processes
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
  Alertix is compliant with industry security standards and undergoes regular third-party security assessments to ensure our systems remain secure.
</Info>

## Amazon Data Handling

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/amazon-data-handling.png" alt="Amazon Data Handling" />
</Frame>

We take special care with your Amazon seller data:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Secure Connection">
    Your Amazon Seller ID and related information are stored securely with encryption.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Limited Data Collection">
    We only collect the minimum data necessary to provide our monitoring services.
  </Step>
  <Step title="No Password Storage">
    Alertix never stores your Amazon password or requires full access to your Seller Central account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Data Isolation">
    Your Amazon data is isolated from other users' data with strict access controls.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Never share your Amazon Seller Central password with any third-party service, including Alertix. We only require your Seller ID to monitor your listings.
</Warning>

## Security Best Practices

Follow these recommendations to maximize the security of your Alertix account:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Use Strong Passwords" icon="key">
    Create unique, complex passwords for your Alertix account that aren't used for other services.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Enable Two-Factor Authentication" icon="mobile">
    Add an extra layer of security by requiring a second verification method beyond your password.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Regular Security Review" icon="clipboard-check">
    Periodically check your active sessions and recent account activity for any suspicious behavior.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Keep Contact Info Updated" icon="address-card">
    Maintain current email addresses and phone numbers to ensure you can recover your account if needed.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Team Security (Business & Enterprise Plans)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="User Permissions">
    Configure appropriate access levels for team members based on their roles and responsibilities:
    - Admin: Full system access
    - Manager: Alert management and ASIN configuration
    - Analyst: View-only access to alerts and reports
    - Custom: Tailored permission sets
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Access Monitoring">
    - Track user activity logs to monitor who accessed what information
    - Receive notifications for sensitive actions like permission changes
    - Regular access reviews to ensure appropriate permissions
    - Automated alerts for unusual access patterns
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  When team members leave your organization, immediately deactivate their Alertix accounts to maintain security.
</Tip>

## Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a security incident:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Detection">
    Our security systems continuously monitor for unusual activity and potential security threats.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Containment">
    If an incident occurs, we immediately isolate affected systems to prevent further impact.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Notification">
    We promptly notify affected users about any security incidents that may impact their data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resolution">
    Our security team works to resolve the issue and implement measures to prevent similar incidents.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Alertix maintains a comprehensive incident response plan that is regularly tested and updated to ensure we can respond effectively to any security events.
</Info>
