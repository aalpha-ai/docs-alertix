## Support Access

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/support-access.png" alt="Support Access" />
</Frame>

Multiple ways to get in touch with our support team:

### Support Channels

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email Support">
    Contact our support team at support@alertix.com for assistance with any issues or questions.
    
    **Response Times:**
    - Basic Plan: Within 48 hours
    - Professional Plan: Within 24 hours
    - Business Plan: Within 8 hours
    - Enterprise Plan: Within 4 hours
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Live Chat">
    Access live chat support directly from your Alertix dashboard during business hours.
    
    **Availability:**
    - Professional Plan: Business hours (9am-5pm EST)
    - Business Plan: Extended hours (7am-9pm EST)
    - Enterprise Plan: 24/5 (Monday-Friday)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Phone Support">
    Direct phone support is available for Business and Enterprise customers.
    
    **Availability:**
    - Business Plan: Business hours (9am-5pm EST)
    - Enterprise Plan: Extended hours (7am-9pm EST)
    
    Access your dedicated support number in your Account Settings.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  For account security issues or suspected unauthorized access, please contact our priority security line at security@alertix.com.
</Warning>
