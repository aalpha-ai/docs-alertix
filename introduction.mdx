# Alertix: Amazon Seller Monitoring Platform

Alertix is a comprehensive monitoring platform designed for Amazon sellers to track changes to their product listings and receive alerts about important events. The application helps sellers maintain control over their Amazon listings by providing real-time notifications about various changes that could impact their business performance.

## Why Alertix?

Amazon's marketplace is highly competitive and constantly changing. Without proper monitoring, sellers can miss critical changes to their listings that could negatively impact sales and visibility. Alertix solves this problem by providing:

- **Immediate notifications** when important changes occur
- **Comprehensive monitoring** across multiple alert types
- **Customizable preferences** to focus on what matters most to your business
- **Easy-to-use dashboard** for managing all your ASINs in one place

## Getting Started

To begin using Alertix, follow these simple steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    Sign up for Alertix with your email and password to create your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Amazon Seller Account">
    Link your Amazon Seller ID to enable monitoring of your product listings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your ASINs">
    Enter the ASINs for the products you want to monitor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Alert Preferences">
    Choose which types of alerts you want to receive for your products.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Monitoring">
    Begin receiving alerts about important changes to your Amazon listings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Ready to take control of your Amazon listings? [Sign up now](https://app.alertix.com/signup) or continue exploring our documentation to learn more about Alertix's features and capabilities.
